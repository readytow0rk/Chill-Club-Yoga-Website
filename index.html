A Bit Of History

Meditation is a practice in which an individual uses a technique – such as mindfulness, or focusing the mind on a particular object, thought, or activity – to train attention and awareness, and achieve a mentally clear and
emotionally calm and stable state.
Meditation may significantly reduce stress, anxiety, depression, and pain, and enhance peace, perception, self-concept, and well-being. How we start ?
It is completely normal for the mind to wander a lot, just keep bringing it back to the breath.You do not need to change the breath, or breathe more deeply. Just notice the sensations in your nose as you breathe
normally. Start with whatever amount of time is comfortable and gradually build up the length of time that you sit. But allow yourself to increase the time by just a few minutes every week or so, to allow your body and mind to adjust, and to build a steady habit without getting overwhelmed or discouraged.
